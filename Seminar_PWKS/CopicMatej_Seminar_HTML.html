Food in Šibenik

Food in šibenik is varied and widespread. From the freshly caught fish and clams to the delicious local meats of all kinds. Šibenik is also home to one of the most famous and one of the only restaraunts in Croatia that have a michellin star, Pelegrini. Sights in Šibenik

Fortress of St. Nicholas

Fortress of St. Nikola is a unique monument of world architectural heritage that was inscribed on the UNESCO World Heritage List in 2017. To get to the fortress you use a local ferry that goes from the main coastline of Šibenik

Fortress of St. Michael

Fortress of St. Mihovila is located in a convenient position at the foot of which medieval Šibenik developed.Archaeological finds suggest that it has provided protection to the local population since Illyrian times. Today , concerts are held on the top of the fortress

Fortress Barone

Barone Fortress It is located 90 meters above the sea on a hill formerly called Vidakuša. The need to build a fortress on the hills above Šibenik was emphasized by many city princes during the 16th and 17th centuries. Today , the fortress has been renovated and there is a caffe and a small park where people come , relax and enjoy the views.



Cathedral of St. Jakov in Šibenik is one of the most significant and beautiful architectural achievements in Croatia and in 2000 it was included in the UNESCO list of world cultural heritage. It was built for more than a hundred years, during the 15th and 16th centuries, and is unique in that it is completely built of stone without the use of binder.During the century-long period of construction, the cathedral was created by changing three different styles: Gothic, Gothic-Renaissance and Renaissance. T he beauty of the imposing dome, which was hit in the Homeland War, and today is a kind of symbol of Šibenik, stands out with its beauty.

Nightlife in Šibenik







Nightlife in Šibenik during the summer takes place on the terraces of the citys cafes and bars among the narrow streets and open squares. So, join in and mingle with the locals. If youre looking for larger crowds — of the clubbing type — attend one of the festivals in the city or surrounding area that offer plenty of different musical genres and opportunities to hang out with locals and tourists alike .
